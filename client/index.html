About Our Organization

Charity Events Hub is a non-profit organization dedicated to connecting charitable causes with community support. Since 2010, we've facilitated over 500 charity events that have raised more than $5 million for various causes including education, healthcare, and environmental conservation.

Our mission is to create a platform where charitable organizations can easily promote their events, and community members can discover and participate in meaningful activities that align with their values.
